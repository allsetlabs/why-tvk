## Caption Writing Guidelines

✅ DO:
- Lead with the STAT (attention-grabbing number)
- 1-2 sentences max per caption
- Use 🔥 for urgency/anger
- End with CTA to WhyTVK.com
- Include domain hashtags + core TVK hashtags

❌ DON'T:
- Editorialize or exaggerate
- Use vague language ("some say", "allegedly")
- Forget the source attribution
- Make it about politics—make it about DATA
- Use sarcasm (let data speak for itself)

---

## Posting Schedule Recommendation

- **Launch week**: 1 caption/day (5 total across the domains)
- **Sustained**: 2-3 per week, rotating between domains
- **Stories**: Daily, mixing quick facts + engagement polls
- **Reels**: Consider video adaptations of key stats (animated counters)
