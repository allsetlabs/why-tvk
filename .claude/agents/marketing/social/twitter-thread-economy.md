State debt as % of GSDP: 26.4% (2024-25)
In 2016: 19.8%

A 34% INCREASE in debt in just 8 years.

Your government is borrowing YOUR future away.

#WhyTVK #TamilagaVettriKazhagam

2/ The debt is driving a FISCAL CRISIS:

Fiscal deficit: 3.4% of GSDP (above the 3% target)
Revenue deficit: 1.6% of GSDP 

Translation: The state spends MORE than it earns on basic operations.

This is unsustainable. This is dangerous.

🔗 Source: PRS India
https://prsindia.org

---

3/ Electricity subsidies alone are BLEEDING the budget:

₹73,821 crore spent from 2019-2025 (cumulative).

That's money that could have gone to education, healthcare, roads — but instead feeds a fiscal black hole.

🔗 Source: TN Budget Documents
https://tnbudget.tn.gov.in

---

4/ Compare 2016 to 2024:

2016 (ADMK): State debt 19.8% of GSDP
2024 (DMK): State debt 26.4% of GSDP

While debt SURGED, what did people get?
Crumbling schools. Overcrowded hospitals. No safety nets.

A debt burden with nothing to show.

🔗 Source: RBI
https://rbi.org.in

5/ Tamil Nadu CAN be India's richest state.

We have the talent. We have the resources. We have the GSDP of 27.22 lakh crore.

But a government drowning in debt can't invest in its people.

We need CHANGE. We need TVK.

A government that BUILDS, not BORROWS.
